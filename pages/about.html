Jihad Vercher

Founder of CodewithJi

I’m Jihad Vercher, a programmer driven by curiosity and persistence. To me, code is a craft—a symphony of problem-solving where every line, bug squashed, and project fuels my journey toward mastery. I didn’t wake up fluent in Python or JavaScript. My path began with late nights, stubborn questions, and building my first app while wrestling APIs. Now, I’m crafting full-stack apps, open-source tools, and ambitious experiments—all “works in progress” that teach architecture, scalability, and patience.

That’s why I built CodeWithJi, a digital campfire for solving problems. No one masters this alone.I’m alslo seeking community: coders who debate frameworks and swap virtual high-fives. I crave mentorship from those who’ve navigated the quicksand. And I’m open to collaboration—internships, project opportunities, or wild ideas—to grow and contribute.

So, whether you’re a company, mentor, collaborator, or fellow coder: reach out. Let’s debug gaps, turn “unfinished” into “what’s next,” and code something meaningful. Coffee’s on me. The code? That’s on us.
